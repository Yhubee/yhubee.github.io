Publications, Drafts, and Outputs

Even as my portfolio grows, I organize my writing and research outputs around themes of human autonomy, trust calibration, adaptive explanations, and sociotechnical fairness. Below are current areas of focus with room to expand as manuscripts progress.

Working Papers & Drafts

  • Trust drift across repeated human–AI interactions and autonomy preservation in AI-guided advising systems.
  • Adaptive explanation schemas calibrated to user cognition, cultural context, and uncertainty signals.
  • Sociotechnical fairness frameworks for AI-supported decision systems in education and accessibility contexts.

Research in Progress

  • Longitudinal measurement methods for autonomy and self-efficacy in AI-mediated decision-making.
  • Interface cues, transparency structures, and expectation management for perceived fairness in digital services.
  • Data ethics and interpretability lessons from CSR annotation, ICT4D research, and behavioral analysis work.

Public-Facing Writing

I publish accessible explanations of AI ethics, HCI principles, and trust-aware design to support early-career technologists and builders worldwide, translating research insights into actionable practice.
